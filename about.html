Our Story

A neighborhood salon, with serious craft.

Tucked on Pearl Road in Parma Heights, Golden Honey Nails is where regulars come for a clean classic mani — and where first-timers discover what a good pedicure actually feels like.

We strive to provide exceptional customer service. We take pride in our work — and pride in serving our customers — making sure they leave feeling sassy.

— The Golden Honey Nails team

What sets us apart

Three things we never compromise on.

Craft

From a perfectly square free-edge to a hand-painted accent nail, we sweat the small stuff. Your set should look as good close-up as it does across the room.

Cleanliness

Tools are sanitized between every guest. Files and buffers are single-use. The space is kept tidy because that’s how we’d want it.

Hospitality

Every chair is unhurried. We listen, we suggest, and we make sure you walk out feeling — yes — sassy.
